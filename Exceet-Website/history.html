2000

Creation

Exceet Secure Solutions started in 2000 as an IT cybersecurity company.

The main business was to create secure processes in third party companies and create our own timestamp and signature ecosystem.

Today the main focus is security in IoT and IT.

2014

bought by Lucom GmbH..

Lucom becomes a subcompany of the Exceet Group SCA.

2015

new on board: Christian Schmitz

In 2015 Christian Schmitz joined the board of the Exceet Secure Solutions GmbH.

2016

leave the board: Christian Methe

In 2016 Christian Methe, former CEO, left the board of the Exceet Secure Solutions GmbH.

2016

Qualified timestamp

In 2016 the German Bundesnetzagentur qualified Exceet Secure Solutions for the highest security timestamp standard.
That means that Exceet is now able to host its own timestamp server.

2016

fusion: eSS AG to eSS Deutschland GmbH

The Exceet Secure Solutions AG fuzed with the Exceet Secure Solutions Deutschland GmbH.

2016

fusion: eSS Deutschland GmbH too eSS GmbH

On the same day as the previous fusion, Exceet Secure Solutions Deutschland GmbH also fuzed with the Exceet Secure Solutions GmbH, becoming one of Germany's biggest players in IT security.

2021

Lucom GmbH.. sold exceet

In 2021 the Lucom GmbH sold Exceet Secure Solutions GmbH to the Utimaco IS GmbH.

2021

fusion: Utimaco IS GmbH

The Utimaco IS GmbH is one of the biggest cybersecurity companies in europe, after fuzing with the Exceet Secure Solutions GmbH in 2021.
