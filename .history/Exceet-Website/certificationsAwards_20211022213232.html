Teletrust-Initiative "IT Security Made in EU

In the wake of the German EU presidency in 2020 and because of the ongoing debate about European digital independence, TeleTrust established the trust seal "IT Security made in EU".
This new seal adds to the already existing "IT Security made in Germany" and shows governments and companies we develop and operate to the highest standards.

Teletrust-Initiative "IT Security Made in EU

In the wake of the German EU presidency in 2020 and because of the ongoing debate about European digital independence, TeleTrust established the trust seal "IT Security made in EU".
This new seal adds to the already existing "IT Security made in Germany" and shows we can be trusted with projects involving digital high security environments.
